The Tint Pros Classic Series Window Tint

This series is the popular choice among drivers who want the Classic look, but also want a major step up in performance characteristics that go beyond just color. With a unique metal alloy bonded to a special dyed layer, the Comfort series gives you higher heat rejection and additional color options. Plus, since metal is impervious to fading, the sputtered metal/dye construction means the Comfort series has even greater color stability than conventional dyed films.

State-of-the-Art Plotting System

The Tint Pros remains family owned & operated and is not willing to compromise their integrity and quality by creating a production-based tint shop. Our state-of-the-art, ultra clean facility is designed exclusively for automotive tinting. We utilize our two state-of-the-art computer-plotting systems that digitally cut film patterns for graphics (logos, decorative designs, etc.). Our programs have the ability to cut precise patterns for each car specific to its year and model. We also have glass throughout the installation bay to cut film by hand when necessary so there is never cutting on the vehicle’s glass, reducing unnecessary cooling costs. TTP can also install clear anti-graffiti, safety and security films to protect glass, people, and merchandise.

Quality Overrides Efficiency

To achieve precise accuracy for the highest quality tint job, The Tint Pros chooses the best possible technique for each situation. Due to over three decades of experience and commitment to excellence, the “one size fits all” philosophy is not allowed for our client’s cars. The highest quality system in each part of the tinting process is used and TTP has only the industries best window film installers. Since 1992, The Tint Pros have been using computer systems to cut tint graphics, then when technology finally reached our quality of pattern cutting we started using the most advanced film cutting system to cut window patterns for automobiles.
