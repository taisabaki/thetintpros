about us

who we are

The Tint Pros’ 3,000 sq. ft. facility/installation center is located at 16582 Gothard St. Suite Q, in Huntington Beach. This location is utilized exclusively for the sales, consultation, demonstration, and installation of window film. The impressive, 1,600 sq. ft. showroom enable the staff to isolate each individual client to areas most appropriate for their needs; residential, commercial, automotive, safety/security, and development.

The corporate office is located at 7391 Warner Ave. Suite H, in Huntington Beach. Our commercial and residential operations are based out of this office, therefore it is not open to the public. For daily inquiries or to visit TTP in person, visit our facility at 16582 Gothard St.

inside TTP

Inside THE TINT PROS TM (TTP), Family Owned and operated by Lonnie and Greg Earl. Lonnie began tinting in 1979, while pursuing an Engineering career in Aerospace. In 1987, Lonnie opened a new shop on Talbert Ave in Huntington Beach. Lonnie continued in his Senior Engineer position with McDonnald Douglas/Boeing Corporation, while simultaneously operating The Tint Pros and tinting into late hours of the night and weekends.

In 2002, due to down sizing at the Boeing Corporation, Lonnie was faced with an extremely difficult decision of relocating his family out of state, in addition to closing The Tint Pros. The alternative was to let go of his Senior Engineer position and take The Tint Pros from his part- time passion to a full-time career. This was a difficult decision, as Lonnie found enjoyment and satisfaction in both vastly different careers that he had simultaneously for about 20 years.

The decision to pursue their passion of service and customer satisfaction would produce over a 1000% growth over the next few years for The Tint Pros. This phenomenal growth and unmatched reputation also got the attention of the World’s largest film manufacturer (Solutia/CP Films.). Solutia, subsequently selected The Tint Pros as their exclusive dealer of their elite, state-of-the-art product lines, Vista (Residential and Commercial applications) and Formula One (Automotive).

Greg, Lonnie’s son, grew up in the shop helping with various parts of the business. As he got older, he became more and more involved with the business. Greg was in high school when his father decided to make The Tint Pros full time so Greg arranged his school schedule so he could tint after school and weekends. Greg’s passion for tinting and marketing proved to be a perfect match. He went on to become one of the best tinters in the country and his marketing ideas brought a new feel to Lonnie’s current business and strong reputation. Now with the father-son team, The Tint Pros has unmatched quality and customer service.
